# Session Zero Guide
## Building the Foundation Before the Adventure Begins
*[Full content in v1.0 — summary below]*

Session Zero occurs before the first adventure session. It is mandatory and non-negotiable.

## The Five Activities of Session Zero (45 minutes)

**1. The Name Circle (5 min)** — Everyone says their name + one word describing something they're good at. No wrong answers.

**2. Group Agreements (10 min)** — Co-create group norms using the Group Agreements Template. Post visibly every session.

**3. Safety Tools Introduction (10 min)** — Introduce X-Card, Open Door, PAUSE Signal, and STOP Word. Practice using the X-Card at least once.

**4. Character Archetype Exploration (15 min)** — Using the Archetype Guide, students choose or begin choosing their characters. No pressure to finalize today.

**5. World-Building Invitation (5 min)** — Students add one detail to the world (a place name, a custom, a creature). This is their world too.

## What Session Zero Accomplishes

- Establishes psychological safety before vulnerability is required
- Creates group ownership of agreements
- Gives students agency over their character and world
- Introduces safety tools before they're needed in an emergency
- Allows the facilitator to observe initial group dynamics before therapeutic play begins
