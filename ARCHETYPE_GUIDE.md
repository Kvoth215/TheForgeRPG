# Character Archetype Guide

## The Five Archetypes

### 🛡️ The Guardian — "I will keep them safe."
HEART: Resolve 4, Teamwork 4, Agility 3, Head 2, Empathy 2
Special: *Stand Firm* — Prevent a companion from losing Calm Points once/session
Best for: Protective students working on allowing vulnerability

### 🔍 The Seeker — "There's always more to discover."
HEART: Head 4, Empathy 3, Resolve 3, Agility 2, Teamwork 2
Special: *Read the Room* — Advantage on Empathy checks for NPC emotions
Best for: Analytical students working on social risk-taking

### 🎭 The Trickster — "There's always another way."
HEART: Agility 4, Head 3, Empathy 3, Resolve 2, Teamwork 2
Special: *Unexpected Solution* — Bypass one obstacle creatively once/session
Best for: Students using humor defensively; working on authentic connection

### 💚 The Healer — "Let me help."
HEART: Empathy 5, Teamwork 3, Head 3, Resolve 2, Agility 1
Special: *Restore* — Help a companion regain 2 Calm Points once/session
Best for: Highly empathic students working on their own self-advocacy

### 📚 The Scholar — "Knowledge is power."
HEART: Head 5, Resolve 3, Empathy 2, Teamwork 2, Agility 2
Special: *Prepared* — Declare prior research; gain Advantage on a Head check once/session
Best for: High-cognitive students working on tolerating uncertainty and trusting the group

---

## Facilitator Note
Let students choose their own archetype. The act of choosing requires self-reflection. Growth Stories over 8 sessions allow every archetype to develop in all five HEART areas.
