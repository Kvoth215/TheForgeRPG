# FORGE RPG: Program Summary for Administrators

**What it is:** A free, research-informed therapeutic role-playing game program for middle school students needing Tier 2 social-emotional support.

**Who delivers it:** Your Licensed School Social Worker — no outside contractors needed.

**Who participates:** 3-5 students per group, identified through PBIS screening or referral, grades 6-8.

**Time required:** 45 minutes/week per group; 8 weeks per module.

**Cost:** ~$45/group for materials. Program is free and open-source.

## Why It Works

| Evidence | Source |
|---|---|
| 11-percentile-point academic gains from SEL | Durlak et al., 2011 (n=270,000) |
| TTRPG intervention improved social competence vs. control | Lim & Lee, 2021 (RCT) |
| Game-based format increases engagement in resistant students | Mayer, 2019 |

## Regulatory Alignment

✅ ESSA Title IV-A eligible | ✅ PA Safe Schools compliant | ✅ MTSS Tier 2 | ✅ PBIS compatible | ✅ Chapter 14 IEP compatible

Students who previously avoided peers willingly collaborating. Students labeled "behavioral problems" demonstrating empathy and problem-solving. Social workers with measurable outcome data. A Tier 2 intervention that students actually want to attend.
