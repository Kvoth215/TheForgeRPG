# Parent/Guardian Notification Letter

[SCHOOL LETTERHEAD]

Dear Parent or Guardian of ________________________,

I am writing to let you know that your child has been recommended for a special program called **FORGE RPG** — a social skills group that uses cooperative storytelling and games to help students build friendships, manage emotions, and work well with others.

## What Will Happen in the Group?

Your child will join a small group of 3–5 students, led by me (the school social worker). Together, we'll create and play characters in an adventure story — like a book where everyone helps write the ending. Through the story, students practice real skills: listening, working as a team, understanding how others feel, and handling challenges calmly.

Sessions are 45 minutes once a week for 8 weeks.

## Why Was My Child Recommended?

Many students benefit from extra practice with social skills. Being recommended for this group means your child's teacher or counselor noticed they might enjoy and grow from this kind of structured, fun practice. It is not a punishment or a sign that something is wrong.

## Is This Safe?

Yes. I am a licensed school social worker. The program has specific safety protocols. Students are never forced to share anything personal, and they can always take a break if they feel uncomfortable.

## Is This Research-Based?

Yes. This program is grounded in published research showing that game-based social skill programs help students build better friendships, manage emotions, and even improve academically.
