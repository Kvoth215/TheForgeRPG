# FORGE RPG Quick Reference Card

## HEART Attributes
- **H**ead — Thinking & planning
- **E**mpathy — Understanding others
- **A**gility — Quick action
- **R**esolve — Emotional strength
- **T**eamwork — Working together

## How to Roll
1. Describe what you're doing
2. Roll a d20
3. Add your HEART score
4. Meet or beat the target number

## Calm Points
🔋🔋🔋🔋🔋 = 5 = Calm and focused
🔋🔋 = 2 = Disadvantage on some rolls
⬜ = 0 = Need a moment — ask for help

## Help Action
Say how you help → Your ally gets Advantage (no roll needed)

## Safety Words
**Tap the X-Card** = stop this, no explanation needed
**"Open Door"** = I need to step out
**"SHIELD"** = stop everything right now
