# FORGE RPG Theory of Change
## Logic Model for Program Evaluation and Grant Applications

## Problem Statement

Middle school students (ages 11-15) experiencing social-emotional difficulties face compounding risks: peer rejection, academic disengagement, mental health deterioration, and long-term health consequences (Felitti et al., 1998; Hoagwood et al., 2007). School-based Tier 2 interventions are needed, but engagement barriers — stigma, developmental resistance to explicit instruction, and lack of peer context — limit effectiveness of traditional approaches.

## Target Population

Students in grades 6-8 identified through PBIS screening (SRSS ≥4) or teacher/counselor referral as experiencing:
- Social skill deficits
- Peer relationship difficulties
- Emotional dysregulation
- Social anxiety or withdrawal
- Low self-efficacy

## INPUTS → ACTIVITIES → OUTPUTS → OUTCOMES

### Inputs
- Licensed school social worker (facilitator)
- FORGE RPG materials (free; printing ~$45/group)
- School scheduling support (45 min/week)
- Parent notification and consent process

### Activities
- 8-session adventure modules (Module 1: The Bridge Between Worlds)
- CASEL-aligned debriefs after every session
- Calm Points emotion regulation mechanic every session
- Safety tool suite (X-Card, Open Door, PAUSE)
- Progress monitoring every 4 sessions
- Parent communication at program start and end

### Outputs
- Students served (target: [X]/year)
- Sessions delivered (target: 8 sessions/group)
- Groups completed (target: [X]/year)
- Progress monitoring data collected

### Short-Term Outcomes (3-6 months)
- Improved FORGE Student Growth Scale scores (SEL competencies)
- Increased willingness to collaborate with peers
- Improved emotion identification and regulation
- Increased school engagement (attendance, participation)
- Reduced behavioral incidents for participating students

### Long-Term Outcomes (1-3 years)
- Improved peer relationship quality (sociometric data)
- Reduced office discipline referrals
- Improved academic performance
- Reduced need for Tier 3 individual services
- Improved teacher-reported social behavior

## Measurement

| Outcome | Measure | Timing | Data Source |
|---|---|---|---|
| SEL competency | FORGE Growth Scale | Pre/post each module | Facilitator |
| Self-awareness | Student self-report | Pre/post | Student |
| Peer relationships | Teacher survey | Pre/post | Teacher |
| School behavior | ODR count | Quarterly | Admin |
| Academic performance | GPA, attendance | Semester | Admin |
